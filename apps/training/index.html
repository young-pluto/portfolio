Day 1: Chest, Shoulders (Side), Triceps, Abs

Flat Smith Machine Bench Press 4 sets × 8-12 reps
Incline Dumbbell Flyes 4 sets × 10-12 reps (superset last 2 sets with Push-Ups)
Single-Arm Overhead Dumbbell Triceps Extension 4 sets × 10-12 reps
Single-Arm Cable Lateral Raises 3 sets × 12-15 reps
Seated Ab Crunch Machine 3 sets × 15-20 reps

Day 2: Back, Biceps, Rear Delts

Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown 4 sets × 10-12 reps
Straight-Arm Cable Lat Pushdown 3 sets × 15 reps
Incline Dumbbell Bicep Curls 4 sets × 10-12 reps
Bent-Over Cable Rear Delt Flyes 3 sets × 10-15 reps

Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves), Shoulders (Side)

Smith Machine Squat 4 sets × 6-8 reps
Walking Lunges 3 sets × 10 steps each leg
Standing Calf Raises 3 sets × 15-20 reps
Seated Leg Curl Machine 3 sets × 15-20 reps
Dumbbell Lateral Raises 3 sets × 15-17 reps

Day 4: Chest, Triceps, Abs

Incline Dumbbell Bench Press 4 sets × 8-10 reps
High-to-Low Cable Chest Flyes 3 sets × 15-20 reps
Bodyweight Dips 3 sets × AMRAP
Cable Triceps Pushdowns 3-4 sets
Ab Wheel Rollouts 2-3 sets

Day 5: Back, Biceps, Rear Delts

Seated Cable Row On Hold
Preacher Curl (EZ-Bar or Machine) 2 sets
Rope Cable Hammer Curls 2 sets
Standing Cable Rear Delt Flyes 3 sets
Bodyweight Pull-Ups 3 sets × AMRAP
Assisted Pull-Ups (Machine or Bands) 3 sets × 10-15 reps

Day 6: Legs (Glutes, Hamstrings, Quads), Shoulders (Side), Abs

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts 3-4 sets
Leg Press Machine 3-4 sets
Bulgarian Split Squats 3 sets per leg
Leg Extensions 3 sets
Seated Dumbbell Lateral Raises 3 sets
Core/Abs 2 sets (your choice: planks, cable crunches, etc.)
